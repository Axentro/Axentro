Slow Transactions


You can send tokens from your wallet to another wallet in slow mode which goes through proof of work concensus.


SUSHI Transactions


Given I have 10 SUSHI in my wallet
And my friend has 0 SUSHI in their wallet
When I send them 5 SUSHI at the fee of 0.0001
Then my wallet should be debited the sending amount plus fee leaving me 4.9999 SUSHI
And my friends wallet should have a balance of 5 SUSHI
And there should be 0 rejected transactions

Custom Tokens


Given I have 10 SUSHI in my wallet
When I create the custom token KINGS
with the amount 100000 and the fee 1
Then my wallet should be debited the fee leaving me 9 SUSHI
And my wallet should have a balance of 100000 KINGS
And there should be 0 rejected transactions

Custom Token Transactions


Given I have 10 SUSHI in my wallet
And I create 10 KINGS in my wallet (at fee 0.1)
And my friend has 0 SUSHI in their wallet
When I send them 5 KINGS at the fee of 0.0001
Then my wallet should be debited the sent amount + sending fee + token creation fee, leaving me 9.8999 SUSHI
And my wallet should have a balance of 5 KINGS
And my friends wallet should have a balance of 0 SUSHI
And my friends wallet should have a balance of 5 KINGS
And there should be 0 rejected transactions
